Let's make some French Toast!

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 4, large
  • Milk: 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla Extract: 1/2 tsp
  • Cinnamon: 1/4 tsp, ground
  • Butter: 2 tbsp
  • King's Hawaiian® Sweet Bread: 4 slices, 1-inch thick
  • Powdered Sugar: 1 tbsp
  • Maple Syrup: 2 tbsp

Directions

  1. In shallow mixing bowl, whisk together the Eggs, Milk, Vanilla Extract, and Cinnamon.
  2. Place Butter in frying pan, set to low-medium heat.
  3. Quickly dip Slices of Bread (do not soak) in egg mixture and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Keep egg mixture stirred and spices well-blended.
  5. Sprinkle with Powdered Sugar and serve with warm Maple Syrup.
