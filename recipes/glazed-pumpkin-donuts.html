Ingredients

  • Donuts
    • All-Purpose Flour: 2 cups
    • Brown Sugar: 1/2 cup, packed
    • Baking Powder: 1 & 1/2 tsp
    • Pumpkin Pie Spice: 1 & 1/2 tsp
    • Salt: 1/2 tsp
    • Baking Soda: 1/4 tsp
    • Pumpkin Puree: 1 cup
    • Eggs: 2
    • Milk: 1/4 cup
    • Butter: 1/4 cup, softened

  • Glaze
    • Confectioner's Sugar: 1 & 1/2 cups
    • Butter: 1/4 cup, melted
    • Water: 2 tbsp
    • Vanilla Extract: 1 tsp

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325℉/165℃. Use parchment paper to line the baking sheet.
  2. Stir All-Purpose Flour, Brown Sugar, Baking Powder, Pumpkin Pie Spice, Salt, and Baking Soda together in a bowl.
  3. Add Pumpkin Puree, Eggs, Milk, and Softened Butter. Use electric hand mixer on low speed to beat mixture until combined into a batter; spoon into a donut pan.
  4. Bake 8 - 10 minutes or until donuts spring back when touched.
  5. Beat Confectioner's Sugar, Melted Butter, Water, and Vanilla Extract together in a bowl until smooth. Dip warm donuts into the glaze and place onto prepared baking sheet to cool. *note: let excess glaze drip back into bowl before placement.
